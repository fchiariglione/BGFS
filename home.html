We Are BGFS

Black Girl Film School is a collection of media experts, educators, tech innovators, and below the line crew all with one common goal of increasing the number of Black women leading from behind the camera.

Tap into our mission
Film School

100% Online, Film School For Girls, Cost never a barrier.

We deliver our rigorous filmmaking curriculum 100% Online and keep the learning experience free to the student. Our courses and programs specialize in technical and creative production areas behind the camera.
